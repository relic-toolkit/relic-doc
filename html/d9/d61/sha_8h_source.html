12 * and Technology Federal Information Processing Standards
13 * Publication (FIPS PUB) 180-1 published on April 17, 1995, 180-2
14 * published on August 1, 2002, and the FIPS PUB 180-2 Change
15 * Notice published on February 28, 2004.

21 * The five hashes are defined in these sizes:
